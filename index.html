Vengatesan Govindaraju

(a.k.a. Venky)

Senior Staff Engineer
Panasonic R&D Center Singapore,
Panasonic Asia-Pacific, Singapore.

I am a dedicated engineering professional with over 10 years of experience in robotics, 3D computer vision, and AI, specializing in the development of innovative solutions that drive efficiency and automation. My expertise includes real-time, highly realistic 3D reconstruction pipelines for indoor environments using technologies such as StructureFromMotion, MultiViewStereo, 3D and 2D Gaussian Splatting, and NeRF. I have integrated AI-based techniques like semantic segmentation and image intrinsic decomposition for advanced 3D scene relighting applications. Additionally, I have developed real-time 3D surface mesh generation and texturing using RGB-D sensors, and I'm currently working on meta features into 3D Gaussian Splatting for enhanced multimodal scene understanding.
In the field of robotics, I have significantly enhanced visual localization and mapping performance through proprietary feature-dased V-SLAM software. My work spans autonomous systems including vehicles, delivery robots, and wheelchairs, utilizing novel algorithms and sensor fusion techniques with fisheye and pinhole cameras, Lidar, and IMU. I led the migration of the HOSPI delivery robot from ROS1 to ROS2, developed ROS2-based adapters for Robotics Middleware Framework (RMF), and spearheaded the development of autonomous mobile robots for construction environments, contributing to commercial applications in facade inspection services. My efforts were showcased at the ISARC conference.
As a proven leader, I have managed cross-functional teams in dynamic startup settings, guiding projects from design and procurement to deployment and testing. At Aitech Robotics and Automation, I served as Project Manager and Tech Lead, developing navigation algorithms for autonomous mobile robots and implementing vision- and LiDAR-based systems for precise operations. I introduced Scrum-based methodologies to improve workflow and collaboration, and I developed APIs to ensure seamless communication between robots and workflow management systems. My hands-on approach fosters collaboration among engineering, IT, and operations teams, ensuring timely project completion and exceptional performance. I am passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful solutions and eager to contribute my skills to advance the fields of AI and robotics.
