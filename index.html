Vengatesan Govindaraju

(a.k.a. Venky)

Senior Engineer
Aitech Robotics and Automation Pte. Ltd.,
Singapore.

At Aitech Robotics, I have designed and developed highly robust localization and navigation control algorithms, for several heavy-duty autonomous mobile-robot platforms, for use in construction and logistics industries. I have provided critical inputs to the projects to guarantee a high degree of navigation accuracy. As the project manager, I have spearheaded a team of engineers to successfully test and develop the omnidirectional robot platform capable of carrying up to 10 tons of load. Through reaserch collaborations with universities, we have developed robot control algorithms for autonomous forklifts and autonomous reach-trucks.
